📊 Key Insights — Year over Year
👥 Attendance Dipped
127 attendees in Tulum vs. 112 in Tampa — a 12% drop. Worth monitoring for Vancouver 2026 to ensure registration pacing recovers.
🏥 Association Segment Strengthened
Association attendees (Medical/Dental + Professional + Trade) rose from 82% in Tulum to 88% in Tampa — the core buyer audience is deepening.
🎯 Senior-Level Consistency
Director+ representation held steady at ~70% both years. The event reliably attracts decision-makers — a strong story for sponsors and partners.
📉 VP Attendance Declined
VP-level attendees dropped from 31 in Tulum to 19 in Tampa. Could reflect the smaller overall attendance or a shift in who organizations are sending.
📅 Smaller Event Managers Growing
Attendees managing 1–15 events/year grew from 58% to 78%. This signals a shift toward smaller-portfolio planners — relevant for programming and sponsor matching.
🌆 Strong Tampa Destination ROI
74% of 2025 attendees would consider Tampa for future events, and 95% either said yes or maybe. Powerful asset for the Visit Tampa Bay host impact story.
